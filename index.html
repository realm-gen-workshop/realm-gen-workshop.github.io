Real‑World–Constrained and Preference‑Aligned Flow‑ and Diffusion‑Based Models

A workshop on post‑training and inference‑time alignment for generative models.

ICLR 2026 • City/Date TBA Hybrid (in‑person + virtual)

About

ReALM‑GEN brings together researchers and practitioners working on aligning flow‑ and diffusion‑based models with real‑world constraints and human preferences. Topics include distribution tilting, reward‑guided generation, discrete diffusion guidance, test‑time scaling, and applications in language, vision, scientific imaging, molecules, and robotics.

Goals

  • Bridge theory and practice of inference‑time alignment.
  • Surface open problems on safety, controllability, and evaluation.
  • Foster collaboration across academia and industry.
